📜 The Divine Legend

The tale of Ghushma’s devotion and the eternal Jyotirlinga

The Story of Ghushma

Devotee Ghushma offered 101 Shiva Lingas daily. When her faith was tested through loss, she remained unshaken.

Lord Shiva appeared, blessing her devotion, declaring His eternal presence here as Ghushmeshwar Jyotirlinga.

Reconstructed by Queen Ahilyabai Holkar in the 18th century — a symbol of eternal devotion.
